Never been to Pittsburgh before?! Why not make a weekend of it! Here are some of our recommendations:

Looking for indoor adventures? Check out some museums!

  • Carnegie Science Center (One Allegheney Ave. Pittsburgh PA 15212
    • carnegiesciencecenter.org
    • Educational fun for all ages!
  • Carnegie Museum of Art (4400 Forbes Ave. Pittsburgh, PA 15213)
    • cmoa.org
    • Who doesn't love art?!
  • Warhol Museum (The Andy Warhol Museum 117 Sandusky Street Pittsburgh, PA 15212)
    • warhol.org
    • Did you know he was from Pittsburgh?
  • Carnegie Museum of Natural History (4400 Forbes Ave. Pittsburgh, PA 15213)
    • carnegiemnh.org
    • History is fun!
  • Heinz History Center (1212 Smallman St. Pittsburgh, PA 15222)
    • heinzhistorycenter.org
    • Local history is fun too!
  • Children’s Museum (10 Children’s Way, Allegheny Square Pittsburgh, PA 15212)
    • pittsburghkids.org
    • For the kids. And the kid in all of us.
  • Mattress Factory (500 Sampsonia Way Pittsburgh, PA 15212)
    • mattress.org
    • Honestly: haven’t been yet, but we’ve heard good things!
  • August Wilson African American Cultural Center (980 Liberty Avenue, Pittsburgh PA 15222)
    • awaacc.org
    • Also a notable Yinzer.

Want to spend some time outdoors, or walking around, or enjoying things inherently Pittsburgh? Check out these recommendations:

  • The Phipps (One Schenley Park Pittsburgh, PA 15213)
    • phipps.conservatory.org
    • Beautiful (mostly indoor) display of flowers, shrubbery, and other garden delights.
  • Pittsburgh Botanic Gardens (799 Pinkerton Run Road Pittsburgh, PA 15071)
    • pittsburghbotanicgarden.org
    • Beautiful (mostly outdoor) display of flowers, shrubbery, and other garden delights.
  • The Strip District (One Schenley Park Pittsburgh, PA 15213)
    • visitpittsburgh.com/neighborhoods/strip-district
    • Visit PennMac and their wall of cheese, grab coffee beans (or a cup!) from Presto George
  • Ride an incline, there are 2! (We like the Duquesne incline:
    • Duquesne Incline: duquesneincline.org
    • Monongahela incline: monongahelaincline.com
  • National Aviary (700 Arch Street, Pittsburgh, PA 15212)
    • aviary.org
    • Maybe Pittsburgh is for the birds?
  • Pittsburgh Zoo and Aquarium (7370 Baker Street Pittsburgh, PA 15206)
    • pittsburghzoo.org
    • Penguins! Elephants! Cloud leopards! More!

Looking to grab a drink? Check out some of our favorite breweries / cideries:

  • Grist House (10 E Sherman St, Pittsburgh, PA 15209)
    • gristhouse.com
    • Craft beer, food trucks, great outdoor space, need we say more?!
  • Trace Brewing (4312 Main St, Pittsburgh, PA 15224)
    • tracebloomfield.com
    • Walkable from our house, has some non-beer options
  • Arsenal Cider (300 39th St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201)
    • arsenalciderhouse.com
    • Walkable from our house, has a few non-cider options
  • Threadbare Cidery (1291 Spring Garden Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15212)
    • threadbarecider.com
    • Where we originally thought we’d host our celebration!
    • Good pizza options, also home to Wigle Whiskey, so some liquor options too
