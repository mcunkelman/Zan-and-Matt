Drinks

To help you toast us, check out some of our favorite drinks!

Old Fashioned

Ingredients:

  • 2oz. Bulleit Rye
  • Orange Squeeze
  • Maraschino Cherry
  • Maraschino Cherry Syrup
  • Grapefruit Bitters

Instructions:
Mix and pour over ice. If you add ingredients directly to ice, start with bourbon or the syrup and orange will freeze.

Chocolate Milk

Ingredients:

  • One large squeeze of Hershey’s Syrup
  • One glass of Milk (Zan recommends 1%)

Instructions:
Pour milk in a tall glass until it’s about ¾ of an inch from the top.
Add one large squeeze of Hershey’s Syrup to the glass (more if desired).
Stir vigorously with a spoon. Stop stirring, wait for milk to stop spinning, stir again to properly mix.
Then enjoy!
